FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of electrical panels does S S EXIM manufacture?

S S EXIM builds PCC, MCC, APFC, VFD, PLC and custom control panels engineered to IS/IEC standards, fabricated and factory-tested in-house before dispatch.

Which switchgear brands is S S EXIM authorised to distribute?

We are an authorised distributor of switchgear and protection components from Schneider Electric, ABB and Siemens, including MCCBs, ACBs, contactors and relays.

Can S S EXIM integrate IoT and BMS with an existing electrical panel?

Yes. Our BMS and IoT engineers retrofit sensors, gateways and control logic into new or existing panels for real-time energy monitoring, remote SCADA control and predictive maintenance.

Which cities does S S EXIM serve?

We are headquartered in Mumbai/Thane with offices in Pune and Bangalore, and we deliver panels, switchgear and BMS/IoT projects across India.

What is the typical lead time for a custom electrical panel order?

Standard panel orders typically ship in 2-4 weeks depending on specification and switchgear availability; expedited timelines are available for urgent industrial projects.
